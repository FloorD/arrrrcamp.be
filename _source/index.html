Topics

Our speakers present a wide range of talks, from Ruby or Rails on the technical side, to inspirational talks about managing teams or communicating, all the way to front-end development and design. We’ve even been taught how to survive family parties!

Our call for proposals closes May 16th, 2014. We're looking forward to reading your talk ideas! Submit a proposal.

A fantastic venue

This year's edition will take place at the Handelsbeurs. This amazing concert hall with big lounge area is located smack in the middle of Ghent’s city center within walking distance of the old city parts and with plenty of hotels, restaurants and pubs nearby. Our infamous public transportation will get you anywhere you need to be. Coming by car? We have an underground car park at our doorstep. Literally.
